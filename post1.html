Learning from My Internship at Apple

Summer 2024 Reflections

Posted by Dharshini Raman on September 30, 2024

This summer, I had the incredible opportunity to intern at Apple, and it was one of the most challenging and rewarding experiences I’ve ever had. Although I can’t share the specifics of the projects due to confidentiality, I can say that I gained invaluable skills in collaboration, problem-solving, and writing clean, efficient code.

Working in a team with some of the brightest minds in the industry gave me a new perspective on software engineering. I was constantly learning — not just about technology, but also about teamwork and communication, which are essential in a fast-paced environment like Apple's.

One of the highlights of the internship was how much focus was placed on the user experience. Every line of code I wrote was with the end-user in mind, ensuring that the product we were working on would offer the best possible experience.

Key Takeaways

  • Learning to manage large-scale codebases efficiently
  • The importance of clear, frequent communication in a remote work setup
  • Getting hands-on experience with Apple's internal tools and best practices

Overall, the internship not only improved my technical skills but also prepared me for working in a highly collaborative environment. I can’t wait to bring these learnings into my future projects!

Apple’s campus was an inspiring place to work and learn.
