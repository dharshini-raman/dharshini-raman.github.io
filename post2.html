Winning at the Capital One Hackathon

A Journey of Innovation

Posted by Dharshini Raman on September 15, 2023

Participating in the Capital One hackathon was one of the most exhilarating experiences of my life. Over a span of 48 hours, I had the chance to bring an innovative idea to life alongside my incredible team. Our goal was to develop a geolocation-based financial planning application.

The thrill of brainstorming ideas, coding late into the night, and collaborating with talented individuals pushed us to our limits. Each moment was filled with excitement, and the sense of urgency added to the adrenaline rush.

The Pitch

After hours of hard work, we were ready to present our application to a panel of judges. I took the lead in the pitch, showcasing how our application could revolutionize financial planning for users by integrating location data with their spending habits. The judges were impressed with our focus on user experience and security, which made all our late nights worth it.

Winning 1st place was surreal. We received not only recognition but also the chance to connect with senior members of Capital One's Engineering organization, which opened doors for further opportunities in the industry.

This experience solidified my passion for creating technology that can make a real difference in people’s lives. I’m eager to take these learnings into my future projects and continue to innovate in the tech space.

Celebrating our victory at the Capital One Hackathon!
